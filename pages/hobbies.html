Hobbies & Interests

Photography

Photography has driven me to climb mountains, literally. I enjoy capturing views and landscapes. Even though photography is an amazing hobby, I also find the need to live life through my own eyes, and not through the lense. But nevertheless, the moments captured are always special and appreciated.
